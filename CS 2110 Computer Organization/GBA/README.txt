Earn Coin To Get Rich

Plot:
You were walking down the streets and saw a wanted list.
As soon as you walked around, you saw the monster that was on the list.
This monster is very good at running away.
But you still get money every time you catch that monster.
After you earn 3 coin, you feel rich and you win this game. 

Controls:
Press Enter to start the game
Arrow keys for the direction
Back space to reset the entire game.

Enjoy!
